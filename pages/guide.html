Bandeja Paisa

Bandeja Paisa is a traditional Colombian dish from the Antioquia region, featuring a hearty platter of beans, rice, ground meat, chicharrón, plantain, avocado, and a fried egg.

Address: CCra. 9 #69A-26, Bogotá, Colombia

Website

Sancocho

Sancocho is a traditional Colombian soup made with meat (usually chicken, beef, or fish), plantains, yuca, and corn, often served with rice and avocado.

Address: Cl 10 #38-38, El Poblado, Medellín, El Poblado, Medellín, Antioquia, Colombia

Website

Arepas Rellenas

Arepas Rellenas are stuffed corn cakes that can be filled with various ingredients such as cheese, meats, or vegetables. They are a versatile and popular snack or meal option in Colombia.

Address: Cl. 39 #21-75, Teusaquillo, Bogotá, Colombia

Website

Obleas

Obleas are a popular Colombian dessert made of thin wafers filled with arequipe (a type of caramel) and often topped with cheese or jam.

Address: Cl. 18 #7-69, Girardot, Cundinamarca, Colombia

Website

Pandebonos

Pandebonos are Colombian cheese bread rolls made with cassava flour and cheese, often enjoyed as a snack or breakfast item.

Address: Cra. 76 #33a7, Laureles - Estadio, Medellín, Laureles, Medellín, Antioquia, Colombia
